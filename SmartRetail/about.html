About Smart Retail System

Enhancing Inventory Management

The Smart Retail System addresses the challenge of manual inventory management by leveraging barcode scanning. Each product stocked in the vending machine is tracked in real-time, ensuring up-to-date inventory records.

Automatic Restocking Alerts

When the quantity of any product falls below a predefined threshold, the system automatically triggers an alert to the vendor for timely restocking, eliminating the need for manual stock checks.

Improving Efficiency

This automated system minimizes operational inefficiencies, ensuring consistent availability of products while enhancing the overall customer experience.
